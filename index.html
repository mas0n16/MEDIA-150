Creative Statement

I am a Media Studies major at Hunter College. I have a passion for creating and visual communication. Through my studies, I aim to explore the intersection of media, culture,
and society, and how they influence each other. My goal is to create impactful content that widens eyes and sparks meaningful conversations. I am mostly interested in the entertainment
industry, specifically business and production in the music industry. I am also passionate about advocacy for human rights and politics. Finding the intersections between my interests
and my studies is something I look forward to exploring during my time at Hunter College.
